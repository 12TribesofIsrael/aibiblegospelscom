Open the book

Why this matters.

Moses stood before the children of Israel and read them their future. Blessings if they kept covenant. Curses if they didn't. The blessings list runs 14 verses. The curses run 54.

The book is closed for most people. Open it, and the conditions read like the morning news.

This sheet pulls the 12 curses that name where we are now — verse by verse, King James only. Sit with them. Then come back to the full series.

"And it shall come to pass, if thou shalt hearken diligently unto the voice of the LORD thy God, to observe and to do all his commandments... that the LORD thy God will set thee on high above all nations of the earth." Deuteronomy 28:1 — the blessing side

The 12 verses

Where the prophecy lands now.

Verse King James Where you see it now
01 Deut 28:15 "all these curses shall come upon thee, and overtake thee: if thou wilt not hearken unto the voice of the LORD thy God…" Root cause. Broken covenant, not bad luck.
02 Deut 28:25 "The LORD shall cause thee to be smitten before thine enemies… and shalt flee seven ways before them…" Mass incarceration. Fugitive era.
03 Deut 28:32 "Thy sons and thy daughters shall be given unto another people, and thine eyes shall look, and fail with longing for them all the day long…" Family separation. Foster pipeline.
04 Deut 28:37 "thou shalt become an astonishment, a proverb, and a byword, among all nations whither the LORD shall lead thee." The byword of every culture.
05 Deut 28:43 "The stranger that is within thee shall get up above thee very high; and thou shalt come down very low." Stranger thriving in the land you built.
06 Deut 28:48 "Therefore shalt thou serve thine enemies… in hunger, and in thirst, and in nakedness, and in want of all things…" The literal description of the Middle Passage.
07 Deut 28:49 "The LORD shall bring a nation against thee from far… as swift as the eagle flieth; a nation whose tongue thou shalt not understand…" The eagle. The unknown tongue. The kidnap.
08 Deut 28:64 "And the LORD shall scatter thee among all people, from the one end of the earth even unto the other…" Diaspora. End to end of the earth.
09 Deut 28:65 "And among these nations shalt thou find no ease, neither shall the sole of thy foot have rest…" No land. No peace. No rest.
10 Deut 28:66 "And thy life shall hang in doubt before thee; and thou shalt fear day and night, and shalt have none assurance of thy life…" Fear day and night. The condition we wake up in.
11 Deut 28:67 "In the morning thou shalt say, Would God it were even! and at even thou shalt say, Would God it were morning!" Depression. The verse names it.
12 Deut 28:68 "And the LORD shall bring thee into Egypt again with ships… and there ye shall be sold unto your enemies for bondmen and bondwomen…" Ships back to bondage. Chattel slavery, named by verse.
