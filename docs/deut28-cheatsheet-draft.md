## Page 1 — Why this matters

Moses stood before the children of Israel and read them their future. Blessings if they kept covenant. Curses if they didn't. The blessings list runs 14 verses. The curses run 54.

The book is closed for most people. Open it, and the conditions read like the morning news.

This sheet pulls the 12 curses that name where we are now — verse by verse, King James only. Sit with them. Then come back to the full series.
